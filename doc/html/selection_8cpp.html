REAL_ calculate_whitley_factor ( REAL_  bias)

Calculates the Whitley factor used in Whitley's selection method.

Parameters
[in]biasGoverns how strong one would like to favor high ranked individuals.
Returns
The factor bias - sqrt(bias^2 - 4 * (bias - 1) * rand(0, 1))/2/(bias-1)
Warning
Bias should be in (1, 2]

std::vector<individual_s> stochastic_roulette_wheel_selection ( std::vector< individual_s > &  population,
size_t  num_parents,
bool  replace 
)

Parent selection method proposed by Lipowski A.

and Lipowska D. in "Roulette wheel selection via stochastic acceptance", 2011. The method is a variant of the roulette wheel where the maximum fitness is used to normalize individuals' fitness and transform them to a probability. The latter is used to decide probabilisticly if the chosen individual will be allowed to get offsprings.

Parameters
[in]populationA vector of the entire population of individuals.
[in]num_parentsNumber of individuals being selected for reproduction.
[in]replaceDetermines if the individuals will be selected with replacement or not.
Returns
A vector of indivudual_s (selected parents).
